2. Personal Data Fractals Creation Interface
AI-Assisted Interface Customization

Revolutionary approach where users create custom interface elements through natural conversation with AI assistant. "I want weather as bullet points and an image" becomes a saved personal fractal.

1 User expresses preference: "I want my dashboard to show weather as bullet points with an image"
2 AI assistant generates live preview while asking clarifying questions
3 User refines through conversation: "Make the image bigger, and add temperature trend"
4 Personal fractal saved and automatically applied across all relevant contexts
